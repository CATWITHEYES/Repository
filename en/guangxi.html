Tourist attraction construction

  The party committee and the government attach great importance to the construction of Guilin international tourist attraction. District tourism development meeting in 2013 was held in Guilin, and instructions: Guilin to speed up the pace of transformation and upgrading of the tourism industry and deepen the development of tourism and cultural fusion, constantly improve the quality of tourism and quality benefits; In order to change from the simple sightseeing tour to leisure vacation tourism, we will introduce a number of high-end tourism projects to create a diversified tourism product...
      Guilin hi-tech wanda plaza officially opened, Guilin general aviation co., LTD., Guilin China resources center and hualun vientiane city foundation in Qintan area... Guilin has cooperated with well-known large enterprises such as wanda, hna, China resources and China electricity co., which have had the largest number of projects in the world.

Upgrading show new results

  The china-ASEAN expo tourism exhibition has permanently settled in Guilin, which is the initiative of the city to build an international tourist resort and integrate into the "One Belt And One Road" national strategy, which is also "changing".
      Whether the transformation and upgrading of the tourism industry in Guilin is effective, the data is speaking:
      According to statistics, in 2014, there were 38.71 million visitors, a year-on-year growth of 9%. Tourism revenue was 42 billion Yuan, up 21 percent year on year.
      From January to August 2015, the number of visitors to and from abroad was 28.263 million, a year-on-year increase of 9.2%. The tourism revenue reached 314.8 billion Yuan, up 20.1% year-on-year.
