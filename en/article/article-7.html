China - ASEAN expo will set up a "One Belt And One Road" special exhibition area

2017-07-16 08:41:36 Source: Guangming Daily

  The 14th china-ASEAN expo and china-ASEAN business and investment summit will open in nanning, Guangxi, on September 12, China's new office announced on Monday. With the theme of "building a 21st century maritime silk road and promoting regional economic integration", the expo will set up a "One Belt And One Road" special exhibition area for the first time.

  In recent years, the economic and trade cooperation between China and ASEAN has continued to deepen, vice minister of commerce Qian keming said at the conference. At present, China remains ASEAN's largest trading partner, and ASEAN is also China's third largest trading partner, fourth largest export market and second largest source of imports. In the first five months of this year, China's trade with ASEAN increased by 16.2 percent, 3.2 percentage points higher than the national average. The china-ASEAN expo has played an important role in the cooperation between China and ASEAN. At present, the expo to promote trade and investment cooperation on the basis of continuously extending the value chain, gradually leading customs, quality inspection, electric power, mining, science and technology, agriculture, deepening communication in the field of humanities.

  Zhang xiaoqin, vice President of the Guangxi zhuang autonomous region government, said that more than 80 economic and trade cooperation activities and 30 BBS will be held in the east fair, with an estimated number of professional audiences of more than 10,000.
